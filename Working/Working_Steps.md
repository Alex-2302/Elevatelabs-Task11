# Working and Implementation Steps

## Step 1: Understanding Phishing
Studied phishing concepts, common attack vectors, and real-world examples of social engineering attacks.

---

## Step 2: GoPhish Setup
- GoPhish was accessed on Kali Linux.
- Admin dashboard was used to manage templates, campaigns, and results.

---

## Step 3: Email Template Creation
- A phishing-style email template was created to simulate an account verification alert.
- Dynamic links were used to track user interaction.

---

## Step 4: Landing Page Setup
- A fake login landing page was configured.
- Credential capture options were enabled for analysis.

---

## Step 5: SMTP Configuration
- Mailtrap was used as a safe SMTP testing service.
- Test emails were successfully delivered to the Mailtrap inbox.

---

## Step 6: Campaign Launch
- A phishing campaign was created using the email template, landing page, and SMTP profile.
- The campaign was launched for test email accounts.

---

## Step 7: Tracking & Detection
- GoPhish dashboard was used to monitor:
  - Email delivery
  - Link clicks
  - Credential submissions

---

## Step 8: Analysis
- User interaction results were reviewed.
- Phishing indicators and attack patterns were identified.

---

## Step 9: Learning & Awareness
- Prevention methods were documented.
- Social engineering awareness was achieved.
